01

Data Storage

All your collections, memorization progress, and personal settings are stored locally on your device using SQLite. We do not have access to this data, and it is never transmitted to our servers.

02

Data Backup

You are solely responsible for your own data backup. Uninstalling the app or resetting your device will permanently delete all locally stored data. We do not provide data recovery services.

03

Analytics & Diagnostics

We collect minimal anonymous usage data to improve the app. This includes:

  • A hashed device identifier (cannot be reversed to identify you personally)
  • Platform and OS version information
  • App version, locale, and timezone
  • Aggregated learning activity summaries (e.g., collection creation, verse additions)
  • Crash reports and diagnostic logs for app stability

This data is fully anonymized and cannot be used to identify you personally. It is used solely for improving app performance, fixing bugs, and enhancing features. We do not share this data with any third parties for advertising or marketing purposes.

All data is transmitted over a secure HTTPS connection. Server-side anonymous logs are retained for up to 12 months and then automatically deleted.

04

Notifications

If you choose to enable push notifications, a push token is stored on our server solely for the purpose of sending you study reminders. This token is not linked to any personal information.

You can disable notifications at any time through your device Settings or within the app. Once disabled, the associated push token is no longer used to deliver notifications.

05

Bible Content

Selah uses the Berean Standard Bible (BSB), which is freely licensed for use in applications. The BSB text is provided by Bible Hub and is used in accordance with its open license terms.

06

Data Deletion

You can request deletion of all data associated with your device through the following methods:

  • Uninstall the app — this immediately deletes all locally stored data on your device.
  • Disable notifications — this stops the active use of your push token.
  • Email request — to delete server-stored anonymous logs and your push token, contact us at sophianas170707@gmail.com. We will process the deletion request within 30 days.

Since we only collect anonymized data, we cannot identify your specific records without supporting details. Please include your device platform and approximate first-use date when making a request.

07

Children's Privacy

Selah is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with information, please contact us at sophianas170707@gmail.com and we will promptly delete the relevant data.
