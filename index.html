01

Data Storage

All your collections, memorization progress, and settings are stored locally on your device using SQLite. We do not have access to this data, and it is never transmitted to our servers.

02

Data Backup

You are solely responsible for your own data backup. Uninstalling the app or resetting your device will permanently delete all locally stored data. We do not provide data recovery services.

03

Analytics

We collect minimal anonymous usage data to improve the app. This includes a hashed device identifier, platform information, and aggregated learning activity summaries.

This data is fully anonymized and cannot be used to identify you personally. It is used solely for improving app performance and features.

04

Notifications

If you choose to enable push notifications, a push token is stored on our server solely for the purpose of sending you study reminders. This token is not linked to any personal information.

You can disable notifications at any time through your device Settings or within the app.
